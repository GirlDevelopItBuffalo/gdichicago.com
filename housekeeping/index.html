Housekeeping

Code of Conduct

First and foremost: all attendees of GDI Buffalo events, including classes, other meetups, hackathons, etc., are expected to abide by the Girl Develop It Code of Conduct.

Student Laptops

Students are required to bring their own laptop for classes. If you do not have a laptop, please contact the Buffalo leadership team at buffalo@girldevelopit.com before registering, and weâ€™ll see if we can work something out.

Scholarships

We have several scholarships available for our students who are not able to cover full cost of classes. If you are interested in a scholarship, please fill out our scholarship application. You may also be interested in speaking to your employer to see if they are willing to reimburse you for the class. To discuss and for assistance with this, please reach out to the leadership team at buffalo@girldevelopit.com.

Requests for Accommodations

If you have accessibility requirements/requests for accommodations, please provide us with at least 2 weeks notice so we have adequate time to meet your needs. Please send requests for accommodations to our leadership team at buffalo@girldevelopit.com.

Refund Policy

Refunds are only offered if the class is cancelled, rescheduled, or moved to a different location. If you believe you may need a scholarship for a class, please request a scholarship first before applying to a class rather than purchasing a seat first - we cannot offer a refund or a scholarship in this case. If you can no longer attend a class that you already paid for you do have the option to offer your paid seat to another prospective student. Please let the leadership team know at buffalo@girldevelopit.com if you cannot attend a class so the teachers / organizers know whether or not to expect you.

Course Certificates

If you would like a printed course completion certificate, please email the leadership team at buffalo@girldevelopit.com to let us know. In order to receive a printed course completion certificate you must attend at least 70% of the classes (e.g. three out of four classes for a multiday class). You will be able to pick up your requested certificate at the end of the last class. If you are unable to pick up your course certificate at class, please make arrangements to pick it up at the next Code & Coffee.

Photography Disclosure

Please be advised that photographs may be taken any events for internal use - e.g. internal GDI presentations. By attending this event, you consent that your photograph may be taken. Please reach out to the Buffalo leadership team with any questions or concerns.

Teaching Assistants

Earning Free Classes

After successfully TA'ing classes, you will be eligible for free classes.

Eligibility requirements:

  • TA one full workshop (ex: full one day intensive, or every session for a four week class)
  • Receive a positive review from the instructor or co-leader

After meeting the eligibility requirements, you will being accumulating hours that can be used toward free classes.

Accumulating Hours

For every 4 hours that you TA, you will receive 1 hour (approximately $10) of course credit.

Example:

  • TA 8 hours of a four-week Intro to HTML/CSS class
  • Receive 2 hours (equivalent to $20) of class credit

Due to the structure of Meetup's payments, you can't use your class credit to receive discounted classes. You'll have to wait until you have enough hours in the bank to cover the full class.
